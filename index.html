Welcome to Mind Masters Liberia Initiative

Mind Masters Liberia Initiative (MMLI) is committed to discovering, nurturing, and empowering Liberia’s brightest mathematical talents. Our programs inspire students, develop skills, and prepare them to excel academically and professionally.

Vision

A Liberia where young mathematical talents are recognized, developed, and celebrated.

Mission

To identify, support, and mentor young Liberians with outstanding mathematical abilities.
