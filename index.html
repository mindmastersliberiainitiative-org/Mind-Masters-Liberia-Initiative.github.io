About Us

Mind Masters Liberia Initiative is an academic organization dedicated to identifying, nurturing, and empowering young talents in mathematics, science, and critical thinking across Liberia.

Our Mission

  • Promote academic excellence through competitions and quizzing
  • Discover hidden mathematical talents
  • Encourage problem-solving and analytical thinking
  • Support students toward national and international recognition

Programs & Activities

• Academic Quizzes
• Mathematics Olympiad Training
• School Outreach Programs
• Talent Development Workshops
